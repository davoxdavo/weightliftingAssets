Common questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Core workout logging is local-first and works without a network connection. Optional features such as sign-in, cloud sync, catalog updates, and Apple Health export need connectivity when you use them.

How do I export workouts to Apple Health?

Open Insights → Apple Health, enable sync, and allow write access when iOS asks. Finished sessions can appear in the Health / Fitness apps. Gym Logbook Pro does not estimate or write calories.

How do I sign in or link Apple / Google?

You can use the app as a guest. To link an account, open Account from the Workout tab and choose Sign in with Apple or Google. Both providers can link to the same account.

How do I back up or move my data?

Use Insights → Export / Import for a full backup (JSON or CSV ZIP). When signed in, finished workouts can also sync to your account. In-progress sessions stay on the device until you finish them.

I deleted a workout by mistake

Open History → Recycle Bin. Soft-deleted workouts can be restored for 30 days.
